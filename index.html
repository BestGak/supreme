About us

We are a friendly team of two highly qualified professionals. Our team boasts extensive experience, covering more than 250 successfully completed projects in the field of finishing work. For the past three years we have been offering the highest quality services, focusing on satisfying the needs of our clients.

In addition, we hold the NOCN_Cskills Awards Level 3 NVQ Diploma in Insulation and Building Treatments (Construction) - External Wall Insulation Boarder and Finisher.

We accept commissions for work throughout England and Scotland, aiming to cover a wide range of areas and provide our skills and professionalism to a national standard. Our office is located in Bognor Regis.

We invite you to contact us with your projects and we would love to help you create an exquisite space that meets all your needs. We are ready to take on all the challenges and provide your home with outstanding quality and an unrivalled aesthetic.
